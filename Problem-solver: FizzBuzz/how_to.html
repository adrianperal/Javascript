Problem-Solver Skill

  1. Understand: Know exactly what is being asked, explain it in plain English.
  2. Plan: Plan your solution, analyze the problem and process the information.
  3. Divide: Do not try to solve one big problem, break it into sub-problems, then, solve each sub-problem one by one.
  4. Stuck? three things to try when facing a whammy: Debug: Go step by step through your solution trying to find where you went wrong. Reassess: Take a step back. Look at the problem from another perspective. Is there anything that can be abstracted to a more general approach? Side-note: Another way of reassessing is starting anew.
  5. Research: Try Google, no matter what problem you have, someone has probably solved it. Do this even if you solved the problem! You can learn a lot from other peoples solutions.
  6. Practice: Practice. Practice. Practice. Try CoderByte!
